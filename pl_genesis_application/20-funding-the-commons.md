# Funding the Commons

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Funding The Commons
- **Prize:** Opportunity to become Entrepreneur in Residence
- **Category:** Special Opportunity

## Challenge Overview

Funding the Commons is a global community working with builders, researchers, system designers, and funders developing public infrastructure for the real world—funding systems, financial access, coordination tools, and digital public goods.

Through conferences, hackathons, and residencies, they create high-signal spaces for people doing serious work, grounded in real-world conditions.

## Prize

One builder from the PL Genesis Hackathon will be nominated for a place in a **Funding the Commons Builder Residency**.

## What is the Builder Residency?

An application-based, co-living container for:
- Deep work
- Mentorship
- Collaboration with peers building real-world infrastructure

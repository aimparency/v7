# AI & Robotics

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Protocol Labs
- **Prize Pool:** $6,000
- **Category:** Protocol Labs Track

## Challenge Overview

Build systems that advance safe, accountable, and collaborative AI, AGI, and robotics.

This track prioritizes safety, coordination, and real-world deployment over raw capability — verifiable AI, agent-to-agent communication, human-in-the-loop oversight, and autonomous systems integrated with decentralized infrastructure.

As agents become economic actors, new standards for machine payments (x402), agent identity (ERC-8004), and inter-agent protocols are creating infrastructure for trustworthy autonomous systems.

## Eligibility

Projects must qualify as submissions to sponsor challenges to compete for track prizes.

- **1st Place:** $3,000
- **2nd Place:** $2,000
- **3rd Place:** $1,000

## Example Project Ideas

### Verifiable AI
- Agents with cryptographic proof of reasoning and decision provenance
- Model attestation systems that verify training data and fine-tuning history
- Audit trails for AI recommendations in high-stakes domains (medical, legal, financial)

### Agent Coordination & Commerce
- Multi-agent negotiation protocols with transparent bidding and settlement
- Agent-to-agent payment rails for autonomous service procurement
- Reputation systems for AI agents based on task completion and reliability
- Swarm coordination frameworks for distributed problem-solving

### Human Oversight
- Kill switches and intervention layers for autonomous systems
- Interpretability dashboards that surface agent intent in real-time
- Consent frameworks for human-AI collaboration with adjustable autonomy

### Robotics
- Fleet coordination protocols for delivery or logistics robots
- Simulation environments for testing multi-robot cooperation
- Sensor fusion networks where robots share environmental data

### Consumer
- Personal AI agents that negotiate subscriptions and purchases on your behalf
- AI assistants with portable memory users own and control across platforms

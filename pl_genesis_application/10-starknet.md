# Starknet

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Starknet
- **Prize Pool:** $5,000
- **Category:** Bitcoin Privacy

## Challenge Overview

Starknet joins the PL_Genesis Hackathon to promote privacy innovation within the Bitcoin ecosystem. This bounty challenges developers to design and build tools, protocols, or applications that enhance financial privacy for Bitcoin users, leveraging zero-knowledge proofs (ZK) and Starknet's scalable cryptographic infrastructure.

The total prize pool is $5,000 USD, dedicated to outstanding contributions in this privacy track. Depending on the quality and diversity of submissions, the award may be split among up to five (5) finalist teams. We reserve the right not to award the prize if submissions do not meet the expected quality standards.

## Submission Requirements

- Open-source repository with functional code (GitHub link required)
- Clear documentation explaining architecture, key features, and how privacy is improved
- A short demo video (3–5 minutes) showcasing the project in action
- A README specifying dependencies, instructions for running the project, and team members
- Submission must be completed within the PL Genesis deadline using the official platform

## Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on:

### Privacy Innovation (30%)
Originality and impact of the approach to enhance Bitcoin privacy.

### Technical Execution (25%)
Quality, functionality, and robustness of the implementation.

### Integration with Starknet (20%)
Effective use of Starknet or ZK technology within the solution.

### Usability and Design (15%)
Clarity of user experience and accessibility.

### Potential Impact (10%)
Relevance and potential for real-world adoption or further development.

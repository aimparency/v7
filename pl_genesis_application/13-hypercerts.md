# Hypercerts

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Hypercerts
- **Prize Pool:** $2,500
- **Category:** Impact Data & Evaluation

## Challenge Overview

Build tools that connect, enrich, and evaluate impact data.

Hypercerts are an open protocol for recording, discovering, and funding impact — who did what, when, and with what evidence. But most impact data today is trapped in spreadsheets, reports, APIs, and isolated platforms.

Your challenge: connect the world's impact data and applications to hypercerts, and build new ways to evaluate and surface it.

## Pointers to What We Want to See

### 1. Data Integrations

Bring real-world impact data into hypercerts.

- Connect existing sources (APIs, sensors, reports) to automatically generate hypercert claims
- Use AI to transform unstructured data (documents, websites) into structured schemas to attach to hypercerts as supplementary data
- Build pipelines that attach evidence, metrics, and updates to existing hypercerts

**Goal:** enrich the open impact data layer.

### 2. Platform Interoperability

Connect hypercerts to existing ecosystems.

- Auto-generate hypercerts from hackathon submissions, grant milestones, or DAO proposals
- Build plugins or APIs that let platforms issue, reference, or display hypercerts
- Integrate with grant platforms, research repositories, or coordination tools

**Goal:** make hypercerts a shared layer across ecosystems.

### 3. (Agentic) Impact Evaluation

Build new ways to evaluate and judge impact.

- AI agents that analyze hypercert data and produce evaluations
- Multi-agent systems that collaborate to assess impact claims from multiple signals (reviews, metrics, external data)
- Interfaces that help communities or AI systems compare and rank impact claims

**Goal:** experiment with new evaluation and governance mechanisms.

## What We're Looking For

Submissions that expand the hypercert data layer, connect existing systems, or enable new forms of impact evaluation. We value working prototypes over polish.

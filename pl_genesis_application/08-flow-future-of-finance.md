## Challenge Overview

Consumer DeFi brings decentralized finance into everyday life.

It turns powerful on-chain primitives into products that feel like modern personal finance and are accessible by everyday people.

No jargon. No manual steps. No "sign this transaction" fatigue. Just intuitive, automated financial systems that users can trust and actually stick with—while retaining true ownership of their assets.

## Common Consumer DeFi Features Include

- Walletless onboarding (email, passkeys, social)
- Sponsored gas — users never think about fees
- Financial actions expressed in human language
- Automation via rules, schedules, and autopilots
- Strong security that's invisible to the end user

## Judging Criteria

### Technical Execution
The complexity, robustness, and quality of the code or architecture — does it work and was it hard to build?

### Impact/Usefulness
Does the project solve a real problem? Could it meaningfully improve lives or systems if deployed?

### Completeness/Functionality
Is the product finished and fully functional? Or is it just a prototype with broken elements?

### Scalability/Future Potential
Could the project scale beyond the hackathon? Does it have a sustainable architecture or business model?

## Tier 1: Primary Targets (~€50k each)

### 1. ZIM — Zentrales Innovationsprogramm Mittelstand

**Why Aimparency fits:** ZIM funds *industrial R&D*, not off-the-shelf SaaS. Aimparency's novel will-model graph, agent orchestration, reflection/memory patterns, and economic autonomy layer qualify as **Erstinnovation** in agentic AI tooling.

**Funding (2025+ rules):**
- Small KMU: up to **60%** of eligible costs
- Max project volume: **€690,000**
- For **€50k grant** → budget ~**€83,333** project (60% = €50k)
- Markteinführung add-on: up to €60k at 50% (separate module, later)

**Eligibility:**
- German KMU (<250 employees, <€50M turnover)
- Clear technical innovation + development risk
- Antrag before project expenses begin

**Application:** Rolling via [easy-Online / PTJ](https://www.ptj.de). Two phases: informal inquiry → formal application.

**Draft:** `meta/zim-projektskizze-draft.md`

**Timeline:** 3–6 months from submission to approval.

### 2. Bayern Digitalbonus Plus — Hohenwart seat

**Why:** Bavarian seat unlocks direct eligibility. Plus-tier explicitly includes **KI** projects. Stacks with federal programs if scoped correctly.

**Funding:**
- Standard: €7,500
- **Plus:** up to **€30,000** (50% of investment → €60k project scope)

**Requirements:** KMU in Bayern, ELSTER-Unternehmenskonto.

**Next step:** Inquiry via [digitalbonus.bayern](https://www.digitalbonus.bayern/) — Bezirksregierung **Oberbayern** (Pfaffenhofen district).

**Draft:** `meta/applications/inquiry-digitalbonus-bayern.md`

### 3. KI4KMU — BMBF

**Why:** Explicitly for KI in KMU. Aimparency = "innovative nutzerorientierte Dienstleistungen" + "Daten- und IKT-Wirtschaft". Digital assistants / intelligent systems are listed focus areas.

**Funding:**
- Standard KMU: **50%** of costs
- **Startups <3 years:** up to **75%**, max **€100k/year**

**For €50k at 75%:** ~€67k project budget.

**Excluded topics:** HR, marketing-only AI, IT security-only, predictive maintenance, care robotics.

**Deadlines:** Projektskizzen due **15 April** and **15 October** each year.  
→ Next window: **15 October 2026**.

**Process:** Two-stage (Projektskizze → full Antrag).

**Draft:** `meta/ki4kmu-projektskizze-draft.md`

## Slide 6 — Sovereignty premium

We will underwrite solutions that command a sovereignty premium.

> **[Visual]** Four properties (01–04): (1) **Privacy by default** — nothing is revealed unless the user chooses to reveal it; privacy is normal commerce (cash worked this way for centuries). (2) **Permissionless by architecture** — anyone can join, build, or transact; nobody's permission needed, protecting against censorship and de-platforming. (3) **Verifiable by anyone** — anyone can verify the system does what it claims, not just whoever runs it; math replaces trust. (4) **Exit is built-in** — users hold the keys; they can leave anytime and take their assets, data, and vote with them; no institution or platform offers such an exit.

## Slide 11 — Why now

Six shifts are happening at once.

1. **De-globalization is splitting the world** — post-WWII order fragmenting; UAE, UK, EU, India, China, and Japan each building their own stack.
2. **AI collapses the cost of synthesis** — generative AI drives the cost of producing anything digital toward zero; verification becomes the scarce input.
3. **Physical infrastructure is the next bottleneck** — software is no longer the bottleneck; physical things, hardware, energy, and materials are.
4. **Everyone wants infrastructure they control** — nations, companies, neo-collectives and individuals all building in parallel.
5. **The agent economy arrives** — agents already pay each other, contract A2A on-chain, and coordinate faster than humans can.
6. **Crypto went institutional** — Fund I's market went mainstream with stablecoins settling $33T a year and AI × crypto leading the industry.

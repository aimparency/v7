# Incorporation Playbook — Aimparency UG (Hohenwart, Bayern)

**Goal:** Unblock all German grant applications in ~5–10 business days.  
**Seat:** Hohenwart (bei Pfaffenhofen a.d. Ilm) — 86558, Landkreis Pfaffenhofen, Regierungsbezirk Oberbayern.  
**Why here:** Lowest Gewerbesteuer Hebesatz in the district (300%), direct Bayern Digitalbonus Plus eligibility, KUS Pfaffenhofen support, ZIM/KI4KMU unchanged (federal).

|----------|-----|

- Triggers Transparenzregister entry

**Cost:** ~€252 Notar + €300 Handelsregister + €20 Transparenzregister ≈ **€572** (+ Gewerbe later)

## Phase 3 — After Notar (days 3–10)

### 3.1 Handelsregister → HRB number

- Wait for email/letter from Amtsgericht Ingolstadt (typically **3–10 business days**)
- You receive **HRB XXXXX** — this unblocks all grant portals
- Download Handelsregisterauszug: https://www.handelsregister.de

### 3.2 Gewerbeanmeldung — Markt Hohenwart

- Via Bayern ID / Gemeinde portal, or in person at **Marktplatz 1, 86558 Hohenwart** (Tel. 08443 690)
- Alternative: central Bavaria Gewerbe portal via https://www.bayernportal.de
- Fee: typically **€20–65**
- Activity: Softwareentwicklung / IT-Dienstleistungen

### 3.3 Steuerliche Erfassung (automatic chain)

After Gewerbeanmeldung, Finanzamt Pfaffenhofen sends **Fragebogen zur steuerlichen Erfassung** (or online via ELSTER).

| Question | Recommended answer |
|----------|-------------------|
| Kleinunternehmer §19 UStG? | **Yes** if expected revenue < €25k in year 1 (no VAT filings) |
| Körperschaftsteuer | Yes (automatic for UG) |
| Gewerbesteuer | Yes — Hebesatz **300%** (Hohenwart) |
| Wirtschaftsjahr | Calendar year (default) |

You receive **Steuernummer** and optionally **USt-IdNr** within ~2–4 weeks.

### 3.4 Activate business bank account

Once HRB exists, send Handelsregisterauszug to bank → full account active.

### 3.5 IHK membership (automatic)

- **IHK für München und Oberbayern** assigns you based on seat
- Invoice arrives (~€120–250/year depending on revenue; low in year 1)
- Ingolstadt branch if you want local contact: https://www.ihk-muenchen.de/

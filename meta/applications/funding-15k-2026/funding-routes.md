# Qualified Funding Routes

Checked on 20 July 2026.

## August Target

The credible routes to EUR 15,000 committed by 31 August are:

1. One small angel bridge.
2. Three EUR 5,000 paid design partnerships.
3. A mix of investment and paid pilots.

Grant programs remain useful pipeline, but their application and decision cycles
do not match the August deadline.

## Prototype Fund

**Fit:** strong. Aimparency is open-source developer infrastructure with a
public-interest case around transparent, inspectable AI-assisted development.

**Timing:** applications for the next class open 1 October 2026 and close
30 November 2026.

**Action:** prepare the application in Q4. Frame a bounded six-month open-source
infrastructure project, not general company financing.

Source: https://www.prototypefund.de/en/

## EXIST Gruendungsstipendium

**Fit:** conditional. The program supports innovative knowledge-based startups
for twelve months, including founder stipends, materials, and coaching.

**Constraints:** an eligible university or research institution submits the
application. Graduate and institutional eligibility rules apply, and the
company generally must not already be founded at funding start.

**Action:** verify Felix's graduation date, current company status, and access
to a willing university startup office before investing in an application.

Source: https://exist.de/programm/exist-gruendungsstipendium/

## EU Calls

Broad Horizon Europe and EIC calls are currently a poor route to the August
target: they usually require a legal entity, consortium, larger scope, or long
evaluation cycle. Reassess once Aimparency has paid validation and a defined
research work package.

## Decision

Spend July and August on direct financing and customer validation. Keep
Prototype Fund as the primary grant application for Q4. Pursue EXIST only if
the eligibility check is positive and a university partner can move quickly.

# EUR 15,000 Use of Funds

| Category | Amount | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Founder runway | EUR 9,000 | Six months at EUR 1,500/month for focused product and customer work |
| Legal and administration | EUR 2,000 | Financing review, contracts, incorporation/accounting needs |
| Design-partner acquisition | EUR 2,000 | Travel, events, onboarding, research, and sales tooling |
| Compute and infrastructure | EUR 1,000 | Model APIs, hosting experiments, domains, and monitoring |
| Contingency | EUR 1,000 | Unplanned validation or compliance costs |
| **Total** | **EUR 15,000** | |

## Release Conditions

Do not spend the bridge on a broad team or speculative infrastructure. Release
spending against evidence:

- Month 1: 20 qualified conversations scheduled or a documented channel pivot.
- Month 2: at least one paid or contract-ready design partner.
- Month 3: provenance workflow used on an external repository.
- Month 4: measurable repeat use or explicit rejection reasons.
- Month 5: three paid partners, or a revised offer grounded in evidence.
- Month 6: choose sustainable revenue, grant, or larger investment path.

## Larger Bridge

For a EUR 50,000 target bridge, preserve the same priorities: founder runway,
partner delivery, product reliability, and legal structure. Do not present a
detailed larger budget until the financing structure and founder commitments
are confirmed.

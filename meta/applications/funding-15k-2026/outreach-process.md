# Funding Outreach Process

## Routes

Use one tracker for:

- **Design partner:** a team pays for a bounded supported deployment.
- **Angel:** an individual can commit quickly after legal structure is clear.
- **Introducer:** cannot fund directly but can make qualified introductions.

Do not mix the asks in the first message. Choose one route per person.

## Qualification

Score each prospect from 0-3 on:

1. **Warmth:** cold, indirect, known, or trusted.
2. **Problem fit:** no evidence through active dependency/coordination pain.
3. **Ability to commit:** unclear through credible budget or angel capacity.
4. **Timing:** unlikely through able to decide before 31 August.

Prioritize totals of 9-12. A famous cold contact with no demonstrated problem
fit should rank below a trusted operator experiencing the problem.

## Weekly Funnel

- Add and qualify names before sending.
- Ask the relationship owner for the warm introduction.
- Send one concise message with one concrete ask.
- Record every contact and dated next action.
- Follow up once after 3-5 business days.
- Close or park records without a credible next step.

## Initial Targets

- 25 qualified names in the tracker.
- 15 warm asks.
- 8-10 calls.
- 2 concrete commercial or investment follow-ups.
- At least EUR 15,000 contractually committed by 31 August.

## Call Evidence

Capture:

- the workflow and current workaround;
- frequency and cost of coordination failures;
- why existing project management or issue tracking is insufficient;
- which Aimparency capability is valuable now;
- objections and missing proof;
- budget, decision-maker, legal constraints, and decision date;
- exact agreed next action.

Do not count praise, introductions, or an application submission as committed
funding.

## Design-Partner Ask

Default offer: EUR 5,000 for 6-8 weeks of onboarding, workflow modelling,
direct support, roadmap input, and a documented outcome. Change scope or price
only deliberately; do not promise unrelated bespoke development.

## Angel Ask

Default framing: Aimparency is seeking a minimum EUR 15,000 commitment toward
a EUR 50,000 bridge to fund six months of validation and product development.
Do not circulate investment terms before choosing a lawful German vehicle and
having the instrument reviewed.

## Human Gate

Felix and, where applicable, Robin must:

- supply or approve names and relationship claims;
- approve each external message;
- attend calls and make representations;
- approve pricing exceptions;
- obtain legal advice and sign agreements.

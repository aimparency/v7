# Community Vote Bounty

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Protocol Labs
- **Prize Pool:** $1,000
- **Category:** Community Engagement

## Challenge Overview

Ship it. Explain it. Let the community decide.

We're awarding $1,000 to the PL_Genesis submission that generates the most engagement on X.

## How to Enter

1. Post a tweet or thread about your PL_Genesis submission and tag:
   - @PL__Genesis
   - @protocollabs

2. Include the hashtag: #PLGenesis

3. Your post must clearly include:
   - What your project is
   - The problem it solves
   - How it solves it
   - Which sponsor bounty you integrated
   - Which PL Focus Area(s) it aligns with: Web3, Crypto, AI, Neuro

Threads are encouraged if needed, but keep it sharp and readable.

## How the Winner is Determined

- The post with the highest combined likes + reposts (retweets) wins
- Engagement must be organic
- Teams may only submit one post per project

## Prize

- **Winner:** $1,000

## Submission Requirements

- Standard submission requirements apply (for the project itself)
- Social media post as described above

## Judging Criteria

Based purely on social media engagement (likes + reposts).

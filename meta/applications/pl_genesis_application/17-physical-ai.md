# Physical AI: Most Improved Physical AI Hackathon Project

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Solo Tech
- **Prize Pool:** $500
- **Category:** Continued Development - Physical AI

## Challenge Overview

This bounty challenges teams from the Physical AI Hackathon to meaningfully advance their original projects as part of the PL_Genesis: Frontiers of Collaboration Hackathon. The focus is on demonstrated improvement including technical depth, robustness, and real-world performance rather than starting from scratch.

Teams are encouraged to build on their prior work by improving perception-action loops, model alignment, and generalization across tasks.

This may include:
- Fine-tuning VLM or VLA models on task-specific data
- Implementing closed-loop policies with feedback
- Extending systems to handle new shapes, layouts, or environments

## Powered By

Solo Tech provides Physical AI Tuning Platform, along with the fine-tuning workflow, so teams can focus on learning and iteration instead of setup.

## Prizes

**Winner:** $500

- Standard submission requirements apply
- Must demonstrate meaningful improvement over original Physical AI Hackathon submission

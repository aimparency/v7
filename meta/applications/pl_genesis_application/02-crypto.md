# Crypto

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Protocol Labs
- **Prize Pool:** $6,000
- **Category:** Protocol Labs Track

## Challenge Overview

Design new economic and governance systems that upgrade how value, power, and coordination work at scale.

This track explores crypto as a design space for collective action — programmable treasuries, public goods funding, DePIN, DeSci, novel markets, and financial instruments that enable new forms of organization and resource allocation.

## Eligibility

Projects must qualify as submissions to sponsor challenges to compete for track prizes.

## Prizes

- **1st Place:** $3,000
- **2nd Place:** $2,000
- **3rd Place:** $1,000

## Example Project Ideas

### Governance & Coordination
- Delegation marketplaces
- Conviction voting or quadratic funding interfaces
- DAO compensation frameworks
- Governance simulation tools

### Public Goods & Impact
- Retroactive funding mechanisms for open-source contributors
- Impact attestation systems
- Community treasury dashboards with spend analytics and ROI tracking

### DePIN & DeSci
- Sensor networks where contributors earn for environmental or infrastructure data
- Decentralized research coordination platforms
- Compute or storage marketplaces

### Consumer-Facing Applications
- Split-payment apps for group expenses
- Micro-investment platforms for fractional ownership of real-world assets
- Prediction markets for local events, sports, or personal goal-setting
- Savings circles (digital tontines) with automated payout logic

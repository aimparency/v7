# Crecimiento

## Challenge Information
- **Sponsor:** Crecimiento
- **Prize:** Guaranteed Accelerator Slot
- **Category:** Special Opportunity

## Challenge Overview

Crecimiento is a movement dedicated to positioning Argentina as a global hub for technological innovation, focusing on crypto and emerging technologies. It connects community, builders, founders, startups, international partners, and public stakeholders with the goal of accelerating technology adoption, expanding the entrepreneurial ecosystem, and promoting a stable regulatory framework that enables responsible innovation.

To achieve this, its work is structured through:
- Training and acceleration programs
- Community events and experiences
- Strategic partnerships

## Prize

One standout team from the PL_Genesis Hackathon will receive a **guaranteed slot in the Crecimiento accelerator**.

## Requirements

The selected team must be willing to:
- Be in Argentina for the full duration of the hackathon
- Participate on the ground alongside the broader Crecimiento community

## What You Get

Through the program, founders gain access to:
- Capital
- Close mentorship
- A global network committed to building enduring companies rooted in real-world impact

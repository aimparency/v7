## Challenge Overview

Impulse AI turns complex machine learning workflows into a simple, agentic experience. It allows developers to go from a raw dataset to a production-ready model in minutes—not weeks. No "black boxes." No manual feature engineering. No "model stuck in a notebook" fatigue. Just an autonomous ML engineer that writes the code, trains the model, and deploys the API so you can focus on building your product.

## Common Impulse AI Use Cases

- **Healthcare:** Predicting patient outcomes or diagnostic trends from tabular records
- **FinTech & Crypto:** Real-time fraud detection, churn prediction, or price forecasting
- **Sports Analytics:** Predicting player performance or game outcomes based on historical stats
- **E-commerce:** Personalized recommendation engines and inventory demand forecasting

## Why Build with Impulse AI?

Impulse is designed for builders who need the power of a Senior Data Scientist without the $200K overhead. During a hackathon, speed is everything. Instead of spending 12 hours trying to clean a CSV and tune an XGBoost model, you can describe your problem in plain English, and Impulse handles the rest.

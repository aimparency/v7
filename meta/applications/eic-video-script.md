# EIC Accelerator — 3-Minute Video Script

**Max 3 minutes. Up to 3 people on camera.** Any EU language OK (English recommended).  
**Speakers:** Felix (primary), Robin (optional 30s), screen demo (B-roll).

---

## [0:00–0:30] Felix — Problem + Hook

"People have big ideas. But between vision and execution, momentum dies.

AI agents are getting powerful — they code, research, run workflows. But they're black boxes. You give a prompt, they do something, and you lose sovereignty over the *why*.

We built **Aimparency** to fix that. It's a visual graph of goals — your aims — where you and AI cooperate. You see the whole strategy. The AI executes the grind."

**[B-roll: graph UI, creating an aim, physics nodes]**

---

## [0:30–1:15] Felix — Demo

"Here's how it works. I create an aim in the graph — 'find grant funding,' 'build feature X,' whatever. My AI assistant connects through the graph. It plans, codes, researches autonomously. The graph updates in real time.

This isn't a todo list. It's a **will-model** — explicit intentions with priorities and costs. Git-versioned. Inspectable. Human stays final authority.

We've got a working product: local-first app, MCP integration, multi-agent sessions. We're not vaporware."

**[B-roll: terminal agent working, aim status changing to done]**

---

## [1:15–2:00] Felix — Market + Business

"AI coding assistants proved people pay twenty to a hundred euros a month for execution help. There are a hundred million developers — but over a billion knowledge workers. We're widening the wedge.

Subscription plus compute margin. Open-source core for adoption. Infrastructure play long-term — the Will-UI for agentic AI, like Windows was the GUI for DOS."

---

## [2:00–2:30] Robin (optional) — Team

"I'm Robin Maier. I founded and sold Mindance, a B2B mental health startup. Felix and I have shipped AI products together. We know how to build and how to sell."

## [2:30–3:00] Felix — Ask + Close

"We're Felix and Robin, Berlin. We're asking the EIC for seven hundred fifty thousand euros to reach TRL eight, scale to a thousand paying users, and establish a European standard for transparent, accountable agentic AI.

Help us build AI that wins **because** it collaborates with humans. Thank you."

# UG Incorporation Costs (Germany, 2026)

For **Aimparency UG** — Felix + Robin, **Hohenwart (86558)**, software/KI.

---

## Official formation fees only

### Cheapest path: Musterprotokoll (standard template)

Best for 50/50 two-founder setup with no exotic clauses.

| Item | 2 founders (you) |
|------|------------------|
| Notar (incl. MwSt) | ~€252 |
| Handelsregister (Amtsgericht Ingolstadt) | €300 |
| Gewerbeanmeldung Markt Hohenwart | ~€20–65 |
| Transparenzregister | ~€20 |
| **Total official** | **~€592–637** |

Single-founder UG with Musterprotokoll: **~€531**.

### Custom Gesellschaftsvertrag (full Satzung)

Only if you need special voting rights, vesting, IP assignment clauses, etc.

| Item | 2 founders |
|------|------------|
| Notar (incl. MwSt) | ~€826 |
| Handelsregister + Gewerbe + Transparenz | ~€340–385 |
| **Total official** | **~€1,166–1,211** |

**Recommendation:** Start with **Musterprotokoll**. Add a separate founder agreement for IP/vesting if needed (cheaper than custom Satzung).

## Stammkapital (not a "fee" — stays in the company)

| Option | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| €1 | Legal minimum. Useless in practice — company can't pay anything. |
| €500–1,000 | Minimum viable for invoices/API costs |
| **€1,000–3,000** | Recommended — covers IHK, tools, notary-adjacent costs from company account |

Stammkapital must be deposited in a company bank account before or at formation.

## UG vs GmbH (why UG first)

| | UG | GmbH |
|---|-----|------|
| Min capital | €1 | €25,000 |
| Formation cost | Same order (~€600–1,200) | Same + need €25k locked |
| Credibility with grants | ✅ Fully eligible KMU | ✅ Slightly higher prestige |
| Upgrade path | Rebrand to GmbH when funded | — |

Grants don't require GmbH. UG is fine for ZIM, KI4KMU, Digitalbonus, EIC.

## Fastest Hohenwart path

1. Check name (IHK München Namensprüfung — free online)
2. Open business bank + deposit Stammkapital
3. Book Notar Pfaffenhofen or online (notar.de) with **Musterprotokoll**
4. Gewerbeanmeldung Markt Hohenwart
5. ELSTER-Unternehmenskonto + KUS intro call
6. Tell me HRB number → grant submissions go out

**Timeline:** 3–10 business days Notar to Handelsregister entry.

## "Spawn companies, why not"

You could incorporate multiple UGs for different ventures (Aimparency, Echo, etc.) at ~€600 each. Consider:

- **Pro:** Separate liability, cleaner grant narratives per product
- **Con:** Each needs Stammkapital, IHK dues, bookkeeping, annual accounts (~€500–1k/year ongoing per entity)
- **Pragmatic:** One **Aimparency UG** now; spin out later if a venture needs isolation
